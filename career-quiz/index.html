Questions before you sign up?

The three we hear most from students and parents.

Is this actually free?
Yes. Registration, the aptitude test, counselling and the printed report are all free. No deposit, no hidden fees.
Can parents come along?
Absolutely. We actively invite parents to the 1-on-1 counselling session. Career calls are easier when the family is aligned.
I've already chosen my stream. Should I still come?
Yes. The session digs into specific courses and career paths, not just the stream-level choice.
