Privacy Policy

Effective 25 April 2026 · Applies to ethames.iyrahub.com

Who we are

This site is operated jointly by Ethames Business School (Raidurgam, Hyderabad) and IYRA. When you submit the career-quiz registration form on this site, you share personal data with both organisations as joint data fiduciaries under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

What data we collect

From the registration form:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (with country code)
  • Visitor type (student or parent)
  • Present course (Science / Commerce / Arts)
  • Area in Hyderabad (or city, if outside Hyderabad)

Automatically when you visit:

  • Page URL, referrer, user-agent string, IP address, approximate location
  • UTM parameters, Google Click ID (gclid), Facebook Click ID (fbclid)
  • Cookies set by Google Analytics, Google Ads, Meta Pixel, and Microsoft Clarity

Why we collect it

  • To reserve your seat for the May 2 Career Aptitude Exam and send confirmation/reminders via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or phone.
  • To follow up on admission interest after the event, with your consent.
  • To measure and improve our advertising — knowing which campaigns generate genuine registrations so we don't waste your scroll on irrelevant ads.

How long we keep it

  • CRM record: up to 24 months from your last interaction, then anonymised.
  • Spreadsheet backup: 12 months, then deleted.
  • Analytics data: per Google's and Meta's default retention windows (currently 14 months for GA4, 90 days for Meta CAPI event-level data).

Your rights under the DPDP Act

You can ask us at any time to:

  • See a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent — we will stop contacting you and remove you from active follow-up
  • Nominate someone to act on your behalf in case of incapacity or death

Email admissions@ethames.ac.in or WhatsApp +91-9703003456 with the subject line "Privacy request". We respond within 7 working days.

Cookies & tracking

You can opt out of advertising cookies by adjusting your browser's privacy settings or by using your operating system's "Limit Ad Tracking" feature. The site will continue to function — only ad-measurement accuracy degrades.

Children

This page is intended for Class 12 students (typically aged 17–18) and their parents/guardians. If you are under 18, you must register with your parent or guardian's consent.

Updates to this policy

We will update this page if our processors or practices change. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. We won't apply substantial changes retroactively without giving you notice via email or WhatsApp.
